In this project I learned how to display images in different projections

In QGIS, after applying the Indicatrix layer, I changed the ESRG according to the requirements.

When projecting the spherical, or even not spherical, Earth onto a flat surface to make a map, distortion is inevitable. Several different features can be distorted in tandem or individual elements can be lost. Conformal maps preserve shape, equal-area maps preserve area, equidistant maps retain distance, and true-direction map preserves directions or azimuths of great circle arcs.

WGS84 Projection

Aitoff Projection

Shape, direction, distance, and area are all distorted, except in the central meridian.
